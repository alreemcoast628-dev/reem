Landscape Design

Landscape design is one of the most important stages in creating outdoor spaces. It involves defining the design concept and the elements that will be used to achieve this concept. These elements include plants and trees, pathways, ponds, fountains, and outdoor lighting.

Planting Plants & Trees

Planting plants and trees is an essential part of landscape design. Plants are selected that are suitable for the local climate and soil, and they are planted in a way that ensures their healthy growth. This includes preparing the soil, planting the plants, and providing the necessary irrigation and maintenance.

Irrigation Systems

Irrigation systems are important elements in landscaping, as they ensure the provision of the necessary water for plants and trees. Irrigation systems are designed and implemented in a way that ensures efficient water use and minimizes waste.
